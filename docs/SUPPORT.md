# Support Hearth

Hearth is free, open-source (MIT), and built by one person in their spare time. There's no company behind it, no cloud bill to subsidize — just someone who wanted a local JARVIS and decided to share it.

**No pressure, ever.** The single most valuable thing you can do costs nothing.

---

## Ways to help

| Option | How | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| ⭐ **Star the repo** | the button at the [top of the repo](https://github.com/0pen-sourcer/hearth) | Free, one click. It's the #1 way other people discover the project — genuinely the biggest help right now. |
| 🐛 **Report a bug** | open an [issue](https://github.com/0pen-sourcer/hearth/issues) | A clear repro is worth a lot. It's how Hearth gets more reliable across the huge range of machines it runs on. |
| 🔧 **Send a PR** | see [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md) | POSIX ports, new tools, new skills — all welcome. |
| 📣 **Tell someone** | share it | If Hearth solved something for you, a post or a link helps more than you'd think. |

---

If Hearth saved you time or just feels like the thing you always wished existed, that's the whole reason it's here. A star, a bug report, or a kind word all keep it shipping.

Thanks for being here. 🔥
